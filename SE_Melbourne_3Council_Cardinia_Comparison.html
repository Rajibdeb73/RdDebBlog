07 / Strategic Insights

Cardinia — Where It Leads, Where It Lags, What It Should Do

Evidence-based strategic positioning for Cardinia Shire based on the three-council comparison.

★ Where Cardinia LEADS (3-council cohort)
  • Citizen SSO / Identity (4/5) — MyProfile + MyCardinia is the most mature unified citizen login in this cohort. Neither Dandenong nor Casey exceeds this at the citizen-facing layer.
  • Largest Digital Investment ($32M) — The TechOne programme is the single largest transformation spend in this comparison group, signalling strong executive commitment.
  • Staff Digital Maturity (4/5) — Activity Based Working model with no fixed desks + M365 + TechOne signals one of the most modern staff environments.
  • Community Engagement Infrastructure — Creating Cardinia (Social Pinpoint) with 43+ active consultations, citizen panels (2025), and LimeSurvey backup is a well-governed multi-tool engagement model.
  • ERP trajectory — Phase 1 live; full TechOne rollout by 2029 will match or exceed Casey's current score (5/5).
⚠ Where Cardinia LAGS (vs City of Casey benchmark)
  • Smart City / IoT (0 vs 5) — Casey's Smart Casey Launchpad (since 2020) deploys IoT sensors, AI, and community data platforms. Cardinia has no equivalent programme — a ~5-year gap in smart infrastructure thinking.
  • Community Engagement Depth (4 vs 5) — Casey's 55-member deliberative citizen panel + 4,600 responses + PowerBI API integration is more sophisticated than Cardinia's current model.
  • ERP Completion (4 vs 5) — Casey's TechOne is fully deployed including CRM and customer portal. Cardinia's is 3 years from full scope.
  • Online Transaction CRM (4 vs 5) — Casey's CiAnywhere includes built-in CRM for every citizen interaction. Cardinia's MyCardinia is still expanding its service list.
  • Digital Equity (none vs active) — Casey's Digital Equity Framework and Lab address inclusion for 5,000 new families/year. Cardinia has no equivalent — a growing risk as its population diversifies.
📊 What Cardinia Can Learn from Greater Dandenong
  • Objective ECM upgrade model — Dandenong's 2023 ECM upgrade focused specifically on "breaking down data silos" — a targeted intervention rather than a full platform replacement. Cardinia's TechOne approach will be more comprehensive, but the silo-focus outcome is the right objective.
  • IT & Digital Strategy as a published document — Dandenong maintains a dedicated IT & Digital Strategy web page. Cardinia should publish an equivalent for transparency and community trust.
  • Multilingual obligation planning — As Cardinia's population diversifies with growth, Google Translate integration across all digital services will become a service delivery necessity, not a nice-to-have.
🌆 What Cardinia Should Adopt from Casey
  • Deliberative citizen panel model — Casey's 55-member randomly selected citizen panel provided richer, more representative input than online-only surveys. Cardinia's 2025 panel initiative is on the right path — maintain it as ongoing infrastructure.
  • PowerBI API integration for engagement — Feeding Social Pinpoint data into internal PowerBI dashboards gives Casey real-time participation analytics. Cardinia should implement the same with its existing Social Pinpoint subscription.
  • Digital Equity Framework — Begin planning a digital inclusion strategy for Cardinia's rapidly diversifying growth suburbs (Pakenham, Officer, Clyde North, Beaconsfield).
  • Smart City roadmap — Begin scoping a Smart Cardinia programme (IoT, smart bins, smart roads) anchored to the $219M capital works programme as the natural trigger for sensor and data infrastructure.
Strategic Summary for Cardinia: Ranked 2nd of 3 (63/85). The $32M TechOne programme, when complete (2029), closes the ERP, cloud, records, and notice gaps — mathematically pushing Cardinia to ~72/85 (85%) — within 4 points of Casey's current benchmark. The remaining gap is the Smart City layer (IoT/AI programme) which Casey started in 2020. Cardinia should use its $219M capital works programme as the trigger to embed smart infrastructure thinking in 2026–27 planning. Cardinia's Citizen SSO advantage (MyProfile) is a strategic foundation to protect and extend — it is the integration anchor for all future digital channels.
